ABOUT US

Fresh Ingredients

Me ensure the freshest Ingredients every day.

Dragon Hunger Express is a Chinese home and office delivery establishment in Karachi that is pleased to offer quality Chinese cuisine with great value for money. At Hunger Express, we keep it simple. Our product and service philosophy is founded upon three basic elements; Quality, Quality and even more Quality. Quality in Food ensures that we get the freshest of ingredients and produce every single day for all of our taste dishes. Everything is prepared fresh to order, therefore no form of cooked food or batter occupies our refrigerators. All orders are prepared fresh from scratch at the time the order is placed. The food is specially packed to keep its freshness and taste intact until arrival.
